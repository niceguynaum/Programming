Program Goals and Objectives:

1. Create a functioning train line with 3 stops.
2. Trains consist of 3 cars: Front Car, Fuel Car, and Caboose.
3. One Fuel car can hold 5 fuel. Each stop has a different distance = different amount of fuel burned.
4. Perpetually running program parameters:
	a. New influx of people at every stop (1-10 people)
	b. Ticket system
	c. Some type of clock
